Schedule 

Companion object Schedule

case class Schedule(tasks: List[(Long, Long)]) extends Product with Serializable

A list of time intervals during which a TaskResource is scheduled to be busy.

Each interval is represented as a pair of timestamps.

tasks

Busy time intervals as pairs of timestamps.

Example:

  1. Schedule(List( (1L,2L), (3L,4L) )) // The corresponding TaskResource is scheduled to be busy between 1L and 2L and between 3L and 4L, and idle between 0L and 1L, between 2L and 3L and after 4L.
